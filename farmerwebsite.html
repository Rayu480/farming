How to Start Agriculture

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Research

Learn about the crops or livestock suitable for your region and climate.

2. Choose Land

Select fertile land with access to water and proper sunlight.

3. Prepare Soil

Test soil quality and use fertilizers or compost to enrich it.

4. Choose Crops

Select crops based on season, market demand, and soil type.

5. Planting

Sow seeds or plant saplings at the right time of the year.

6. Watering

Set up an irrigation system to ensure crops receive adequate water.

7. Crop Care

Protect crops from pests and diseases using natural or chemical methods.

8. Harvesting

Collect crops when they are ripe and ready for the market.
